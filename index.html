Event Overview

Geekathon is a hackathon event aimed at tech enthusiasts, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs. Join us for a weekend of innovation and collaboration.

Objectives

Our goal is to foster innovation and collaboration through technology. Participants will work together to create cutting-edge solutions.

Target Audience

We welcome developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and students to join us for this exciting event.

Event Format

The event will last for 48 hours, with teams of up to 5 members. Judging criteria will include innovation, feasibility, and presentation.

Features

  • Workshops covering various technologies and tools.
  • One-on-one mentorship from industry experts.
  • Networking opportunities with potential employers and investors.
  • Access to exclusive resources and tools for participants.
  • Post-event support for project development and implementation.

Prizes

Top teams will receive exciting prizes, including cash rewards, tech gadgets, and internships.
